WHAT IS HOUSE OF PETALS?

WHAT IS
HOUSE OF PETALS?

HOUSE OF PETALS (HOP) is that feeling of love and collaboration, that raw creative energy that really fuels the art and music world. A flower shop by day and home to Hollywood’s rock and roll culture by night.

The House of Petals holds such precious memories, and what better way to honor “The Icon” than supporting his initiatives and donating 10% of this project in perpetuity.

The House of Petals was a safe space for artists to jam and fans to mix and mingle in RL. Some of the notable celebrities were Tone Loc, Farrah Fawcett, Sean Penn, and Halle Berry and more
